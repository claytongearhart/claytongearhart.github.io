State of American Energy

Synthetic petroleum stands as the cornerstone of America's independence, eliminating foreign reliance for plastics, pharmaceuticals, fuels, and lubricants

Mission Statement

ASFA represents America’s entire synthetic fuel industry. From up-stream production to downstream consumer facing businesses. Our constituent members provide more than 5 million American jobs. ASFA’s mission is to promote the use of synthetic fuels, aiding energy security and the life of Americans by keeping energy production local. We speak for the synfuel industry to the Public, Congress, state governments, and the media. We participate in coalitions and negotiate with regulatory industries to help advance the adaptation of synfuels. All segments of the synfuel industry are unified in our commitment to meet climate goals while meeting the global demand for affordable energy and petrochemicals.

Policy Issues

The synthetic fuel industry faces several critical policy issues:

  • Tax breaks for using synthetic fuels: We advocate for tax incentives to encourage the use of synthetic fuels, which are vital for reducing carbon emissions and enhancing energy security.
  • Public perception that petrochemicals are bad: Addressing misconceptions regarding petrochemicals is crucial. Many fail to realize the integral role petrochemicals play in everyday products such as plastics, pharmaceuticals, and textiles.
  • Public lack of understanding of the widespread use of petrochemicals: We strive to educate the public on the extensive use of petrochemicals across various industries, highlighting their importance in modern society.

Audience

Our efforts are targeted towards:

  • The Public: We aim to dispel misconceptions surrounding synthetic fuels and petrochemicals, highlighting their benefits for the environment and society.
  • Congress: We advocate for legislative support, including tax breaks and reduced regulations, to incentivize the use of synthetic fuels.
  • The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA): We collaborate with regulatory bodies to develop favorable policies and regulations that promote the adoption of synthetic fuels while ensuring environmental sustainability.
  • Media: Engaging with the media is essential for raising awareness and shaping public opinion on synthetic fuels and petrochemicals.

Membership Demographic

Our diverse membership includes:

  • Biomass producers: Contributing to the production of organic materials used in synthetic fuel production.
  • Downstream chemical companies: Utilizing organic chemicals derived from synthetic fuels in various industrial processes.
  • Automotive companies: Benefiting from the development of synthetic fuels with less restrictive regulations.
  • Synthetic fuel producers: Driving innovation and production in the synthetic fuel industry.

Funding

Our funding model is based on:

  • Membership fees: Each member contributes a fee based on their revenue, ensuring financial sustainability and collective support for our initiatives.
